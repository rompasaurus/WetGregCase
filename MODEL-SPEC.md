# WetGregCase — Model Specification

A two-piece **slim** 3D-printed enclosure for the **Dilder Full PCB Rev 1**,
generated by `freecad/wetgreg_case.FCMacro`. The macro imports the real KiCad
STEP assembly (aligned, unpopulated header pins stripped, screen shifted to meet
the front face) and adds a snap-on joystick thumbpiece.

> Status: **v9 — parametric PartDesign rewrite.** Every part is a PartDesign Body
> (Pad → Fillet → Thickness shell → pockets/pads) driven by the **`Parameters`
> spreadsheet** (edit a cell + Ctrl+Shift+R, or add/adjust fillets on the feature
> tree). Both halves are curved constant-wall shells; all parts are single,
> watertight solids that slice cleanly. ~19.0 mm thick.
>
> Feature tree: **BackPlate** = Pad → Fillet_Corners → Fillet_Back → Shell →
> Pad_Shelf → Cut_Relief → USB/USBShelf/Switch cuts. **FrontCover** = Pad →
> Fillet_Corners → Fillet_Front → Shell → Cut_Window → Boss_Joy → Pad_Tongue →
> Cut_Joy. **Thumbpiece** = Disc → Dish → Socket.
>
> Closure: flush lap (tongue + relief, 0.2 mm slip) with a **snap bead + groove**
> running along the lap (bead on the front-cover tongue clicks into a groove in
> the backplate relief). The dilder PCB rests on a **thin side lip at Z=0** (USB
> cutout starts at Z=0 to match); the lip has a USB notch.

## 1. Orientation & coordinate frame

`local = (KiCad_X − 46.70, KiCad_StepY + 88.0, Z)` — **Z = 0 is the PCB bottom
face**; board X 0..97.8, Y 0..42.012.

| Side | Z | Contents | Case part |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Front** | **−Z** | WeAct screen + 5-way joystick | **Front cover** (screen lid) |
| **Back** | **+Z** | Pico W, USB-C, power switch | **Backplate** (board carrier) |

## 3. Architecture

```
                       aggressive front curve (r6)
  Z=-11.40  ╰─╮  screen window (stops before 8-pin) + joystick hole ╭─╯  = screen top
            FRONT COVER (screen lid) · 5 mm boss by joystick (solar conn.)
            −X side extended to wrap the screen edge + screw holes
  Z= -1.50 ═╪═════════════ FLUSH lap joint (shelf plane) ═══════════╪══  sides flush
  Z=  0.00       PCB rests ON TOP of the shelf  ───────────────────
            BACKPLATE (board carrier) — wraps board edge, open centre
            USB-C (+X) · switch slot (−Y)
  Z= +4.90  ╰──────────────────────────────────────────────────────╯  = USB-C height
```

### Backplate (board carrier, +Z)
- Wraps the board edge; only as tall as the USB-C connector (outer Z = +4.9).
- **Shelf under the board** (at the lower edge): the PCB **rests on top of it**.
  The shelf bridges the −X extension so it grips the board on all four sides.
- **Open centre** so it never touches a component (Pico exposed/accessible).
- **USB-C opening** (+X) · **power-switch slot** (−Y).

### Front cover (screen lid, −Z)
- Outer face **meets the screen top at Z = −11.4**; thin 1.2 mm front frame.
- **Screen window** inset by a 2.5 mm bezel so the frame **wraps the screen and
  covers the corner screw holes**; the +Y edge **stops 1 mm before the 8-pin
  connector** (pins hidden under the plate).
- **−X extended by 3 mm** so the wall wraps the screen's overhanging edge instead
  of clipping it.
- **5 mm-thick boss** around the joystick → room for the solar-panel connector and
  the thumbpiece.
- **Aggressive curve** (r = 6 mm) on the front outer edge.
- **Joystick hole** (Ø12) + flush lap tongue + snap nibs.

### Thumbpiece
- Snap cap over the K1-1506SN-01 actuator (rev2-mk2 style): Ø11 disc, dish,
  3.3 mm square socket. On the front.

## 5. Open items / confirm during validation

1. **Screen screw holes / bezel.** The 2.5 mm bezel covers holes within ~2.5 mm of
   the screen edge. If holes sit further in (or you want the exact active-glass
   window), give me the hole/active-area coordinates and I'll tighten it.
2. **−X extension (3 mm).** Sized to wrap the 0.4 mm screen overhang plus a full
   wall; adjust `ext_xmin` if you want it tighter/looser.
3. **5 mm joystick boss** spans X 71–93, Y 11–31. Tell me the solar-connector
   footprint if it needs a specific pocket rather than just thickness.
4. **Pico (5.26) vs backplate top (4.9):** Pico sits ~0.36 mm proud of the open
   backplate centre (exposed, no collision).
5. **Bezel overlaps the screen front** in the model (intended — the frame covers
   the border). The real screen sits flush behind it.
6. No bolt bosses (closure is snap + shelf). `import_assembly=False` for a
   case-only run; `explode_z` for an exploded view.

## 6. How to run

```bash
cd freecad && freecadcmd wetgreg_case.FCMacro     # headless regenerate
# or open the macro in the FreeCAD GUI → Execute
```

Outputs `freecad/WetGregCase.FCStd` and STEP files for the front cover, backplate
and thumbpiece in `exports/`.
