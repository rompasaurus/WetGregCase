# WetGregCase — Model Specification

A two-piece, **flush snap-fit** 3D-printed enclosure for the **Dilder Full PCB
Rev 1**, generated by `freecad/wetgreg_case.FCMacro`. The macro imports the real
KiCad STEP assembly (board + screen + joystick + battery + Pico + …) so the fit
is checked against actual geometry, and adds a snap-on joystick thumbpiece.

> Status: **v1.** Flush lap joint, real assembly import, thumbpiece, and a
> side-toggle power-switch slot. All numbers are parametric (`PARAMS` dict).

---

## 1. Coordinate frame (matches the KiCad STEP export)

```
local = (KiCad_X − 46.70,  −KiCad_Y + 88.00,  Z)          # pure translation
```

- Board occupies **X 0..97.8, Y 0..42.012**.
- **Z = 0 is the PCB bottom face**, **+Z** is the screen/top side, **−Z** is the
  battery/joystick side.
- The STEP (`Dilder_PCB_FullAssembly.step`) is translated by **(−46.70, +88, 0)**
  into this frame, so every case feature lines up with the real components.
  *(This corrected a Y-mirror in v0 that put the switch on the wrong wall.)*

## 3. Two-piece architecture — flush lap joint

Both plates share the **same outer footprint (102.40 × 46.61 mm)** so the sides
are perfectly **flush** — no overhang. They interlock with a lap joint: the back
plate's inner-half wall continues up as a **tongue**, the front plate's inner
wall is **relieved** to receive it, and the outer faces butt flush at `z_join`.

```
   Z=+13.0  ╔══════════════ screen window ══════════════╗  front plate top
            ║          FRONT PLATE (cover)               ║
   Z=+10.5  ║··· tongue top / lap band ···               ║
            ║   [back tongue] | [front relief]  ← snap   ║  internal detents
   Z=+5.5 ──╠════════════ FLUSH outer butt line ═════════╣  ← sides flush here
            ║   USB-C slot (+X) · switch slot (−Y)        ║
   Z=+1.6 ──╫──────────────── PCB top ───────────────────╫
   Z= 0.0 ──╫──── PCB rests on LIP ─────────────────────-╫  lip top = PCB bottom
            ║   BACK PLATE cavity (10.5 mm) — battery,    ║
            ║   connectors, joystick body                 ║
   Z=-10.5  ║   joystick hole → THUMBPIECE                ║
   Z=-12.1  ╚════════════════════════════════════════════╝  outer floor
```

### Back plate
- Tray: floor + perimeter walls (2.0 mm), shared outer footprint.
- **Lip** (`lip_w = 1.5`): PCB drops in (+0.3 mm/side) and its back face rests on
  the ledge at `Z = 0`.
- **Cavity 10.5 mm** clears the back-side components (down to −10.09).
- **Tongue** (inner 1.0 mm wall) rises `z_join → z_join+lap` with **snap nibs**.
- **USB-C opening** (+X), **power-switch slot** (−Y), **joystick hole** (Ø12).

### Front plate
- Cover from `z_join` to the top; **same outer footprint** as the back plate.
- Inner wall **relieved** over the lap band to receive the tongue (0.2 mm slip).
- **Snap-detent recesses** capture the tongue nibs (all internal — outside flush).
- **Screen window** through the ceiling (2.5 mm bezel).

### Thumbpiece
- Snap cap over the K1-1506SN-01 actuator (same style as `dilder_rev2_mk2`):
  Ø11 disc, concave thumb dish, **3.3 mm square socket** for the 3.2 mm peg.
- Mounted on the **back side**, nesting in the Ø12 joystick hole.

## 6. Open items / confirm during validation

1. **Back cavity depth (10.5 mm)** is driven by the real back-component extent
   (joystick body/connectors to −10.1). The joystick *actuator* passes through
   its hole, so if the only thing reaching −10 is the actuator you can reduce
   `back_cav`. The original "+2 mm" brief was based on incomplete info; the real
   model needs ~10 mm here.
2. **Screen side.** Built per your spec (screen on +Z / top, window in front
   plate, 11.4 mm above PCB top). The KiCad 3D model's screen tops out at
   Z ≈ +10.1, ~2.9 mm below the window — i.e. the real WeAct on taller standoffs
   reaches your 11.4 mm. Confirm the standoff height.
3. **Thumbpiece socket** assumes the 3.2 mm square peg of the K1-1506SN-01 (same
   switch as rev2-mk2). Confirm actuator size/orientation; the cap is centered on
   the footprint.
4. **Switch slot** spans the SW1 footprint on the −Y wall. Confirm the slider
   travel direction and that the slot lines up with the actuator.
5. **Mounting bolts** — no bolt bosses yet (closure is snap + lip). The M3 holes
   in `BOARD_DESIGN_SPEC.md` weren't found in this board's drill files.
6. **Import path** is absolute in `PARAMS["assembly_step"]`; set
   `import_assembly=False` for a lightweight (case-only) run.

## 7. How to run

```bash
cd freecad && freecadcmd wetgreg_case.FCMacro     # headless (regenerate)
# or open the macro in the FreeCAD GUI → Execute
```

Set `PARAMS["explode_z"]` (e.g. 25) for an exploded view. Outputs:
`freecad/WetGregCase.FCStd`, and STEP files for the back plate, front plate and
thumbpiece in `exports/`.
